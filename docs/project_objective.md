## Business Use Cases

QuantInsight is designed to support users in analyzing financial markets using statistical and probability-based methods. The platform focuses on helping users evaluate stocks using data-driven insights rather than relying purely on speculation.

### 1. Stock Risk Evaluation

Investors can analyze the volatility of individual stocks using statistical indicators such as variance and standard deviation. This helps users understand how much a stock’s price fluctuates over time and evaluate the level of risk before making an investment decision.

### 2. Stock Relationship Analysis

The platform enables users to analyze the correlation between different stocks. By understanding how assets move relative to each other, users can identify diversification opportunities and reduce portfolio risk.

### 3. Probability-Based Market Insights

QuantInsight applies probability distributions such as Binomial, Poisson, Geometric, and Uniform models to analyze historical market patterns. These models help estimate the likelihood of different types of market events based on historical data.

### 4. Market Scenario Simulation

Using Monte Carlo simulation techniques, the platform can generate multiple potential future price paths based on historical market statistics. This allows users to visualize possible market outcomes and better understand potential risk ranges.

### 5. Data-Driven Stock Screening

Users can evaluate multiple stocks using statistical indicators and probability-based insights. This helps users identify stocks that meet specific analytical criteria and supports a more structured stock selection process.

### 6. Learning and Research Platform

QuantInsight can also be used as an educational tool for students, researchers, and analysts who want to understand how statistical models and probability distributions apply to real financial market data.
