## Statistical Models Implemented

QuantInsight applies statistical and probability-based models to analyze financial market behavior and generate analytical insights.

### Descriptive Statistics

The platform calculates key statistical indicators used to evaluate market behavior and volatility:

- Mean
- Variance
- Standard Deviation
- Covariance
- Correlation

These metrics help measure stock volatility and relationships between different assets.

### Probability Models

The system implements several probability distributions to analyze market movement patterns:

- **Binomial Distribution** – evaluates the probability of a stock moving up or down over a number of trading periods.
- **Poisson Distribution** – models the frequency of rare market events such as large price movements.
- **Geometric Distribution** – estimates the probability of the first occurrence of a specific market event.
- **Uniform Distribution** – models scenarios where outcomes are assumed to be equally likely within a defined range.

### Simulation Models

- **Monte Carlo Simulation** – generates multiple potential future price paths using historical market statistics to explore possible market scenarios.

### Future Enhancements

The current implementation focuses on core statistical and probability models. In future development phases, additional statistical techniques and advanced analytical models may be incorporated to enhance the analytical capabilities of the platform.
